Mindful Meditation

It is the process of being fully present with your thoughts. Being mindful means being aware of where we are and what we’re doing, and not being overly reactive to what’s going on around us. When practicing mindfulness meditation, you observe your thoughts and emotions but let them pass without judgement

Transcendental Meditation

It is a simple technique in which a personally assigned mantra, such as a word, or small phrase, is repeated in a specific way. It’s practiced 20 minutes twice each day while sitting comfortably with the eyes closed

Loving Kindness Meditation

It is the practice of directing well wishes toward others. Those who practice recite specific words and phrases meant to evoke warm-hearted feelings

Yoga Meditation

The practice of yoga dates back to ancient India. There are a wide variety of classes and styles of yoga, but they all involve performing a series of postures and controlled breathing exercises meant to promote flexibility and calm the mind
